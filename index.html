Rachel de Jong

Hi! I am a PhD candidiate in computer science in the Network Science Group at Leiden Institute of Advanced Computer Science, Leiden university. I am interested in computations and algorithms in network science.

Currently I work on the ANO-NET project in collaboration with statistics Netherlands (CBS). Our goal is to quantify the risk of re-identification in network datasets and design anonymization algorithms which maintain the structural properties of the network and performance on network analysis tasks, balancing the trade-off between privacy and data utility. I am also affiliated to the PLANET-NL project (formerly POPNET).

Some of my hobbies are hiking, making jigsaw puzzles, crochet and knitting. I also like cats.
